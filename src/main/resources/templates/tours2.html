Zürich, Sechseläuten

Posted on 4.September.2019

When in April the first flowers and trees start to bloom,

the temperatures rise and the days become longer,

the people of Zurich celebrate their traditional spring festival:

Sechseläuten, or “Sächsilüüte” as it is known in local dialect.
